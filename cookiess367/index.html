Privacy Policy for Epic Tower Guardians

At Epic Tower Guardians, accessible from https://spintowinhub.com/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information collected and recorded by Epic Tower Guardians and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

Log Files

Epic Tower Guardians follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this as part of their analytics services.

The information collected by log files includes:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) addresses
  • Browser type
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Date and time stamp
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Number of clicks

These data points are not linked to any personally identifiable information. The purpose of collecting this information is to analyze trends, administer the site, track users' movement on the website, and gather demographic data.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like most websites, Epic Tower Guardians uses cookies. These cookies store information such as:

  • Visitors' preferences
  • Pages visited on the website

This information helps us optimize user experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and other relevant data.

For more general information about cookies, you can read the "Cookies" article from the Privacy Policy Generator.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It uses DART cookies to serve ads to visitors based on their activity on https://spintowinhub.com/ and other websites.

However, visitors may opt out of DART cookies by visiting Google's Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy at this link: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Third-Party Advertisers

Third-party ad servers or ad networks use technologies such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons in their advertisements and links that appear on Epic Tower Guardians. These technologies are used in the following ways:

  • Automatically collect IP addresses

  • Personalize the ads you see on different websites

Please note that Epic Tower Guardians does not have access to or control over these third-party cookies.
